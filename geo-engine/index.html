What is Geo?

Geo is the world’s first game engine designed specifically for retro computing, making video games accessible to everyone, regardless of their hardware. With support for Windows 7, 8, 8.1, 10, and 11—and future compatibility for Windows 98, 98SE, ME, 2000, XP, and Vista in progress—Geo bridges the gap between cutting-edge game development and legacy systems.

Our intuitive node-based editor, Weave, empowers developers to bring their ideas to life effortlessly. With Geo, you can create once and run your games seamlessly across platforms, from the latest software to the most nostalgic systems. Whether you’re crafting modern masterpieces or retro-inspired adventures, Geo has you covered.
